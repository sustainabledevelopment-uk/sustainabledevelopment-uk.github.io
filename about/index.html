About

ONS is responsible for the development and collection of UK data for the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) global indicators and for reporting UK progress towards them.

This website is for disseminating UK SDG indicator data. We are at an Alpha stage in development, so the site is still ‘work in progress’.

We initially based this website on the US SDG reporting platform and continue to collaborate with the US on further developing our websites.

Other countries wishing to reuse the UK or US SDG reporting platforms are welcome to do so for free. We have developed a wiki which includes technical guidance on how to do this. The US have also produced various training materials. Guidance and support is also available from the Center for Open Data Enterprise (CODE) through their SDG National Reporting Initiative.

If you have any feedback on this website or UK SDG data then please contact us at sustainabledevelopment@ons.gov.uk. The answers to some frequently asked questions can be found in our FAQ.
