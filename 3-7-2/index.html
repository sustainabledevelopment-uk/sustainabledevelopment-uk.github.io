    Indicator description
    Geographical coverage England and Wales
    Unit of measurement Live births per 1,000 women aged 15-19
    Definitions The adolescent birth rate represents the risk of childbearing among females in the particular age group. The adolescent birth rate among women aged 15-19 years is also referred to as the age-specific fertility rate for women aged 15-19.
    Calculations (Live births / Population) * 1,000
